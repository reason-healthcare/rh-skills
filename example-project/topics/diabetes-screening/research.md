## Background

Diabetes screening is recommended for adults with risk factors, using fasting glucose, HbA1c, or oral glucose tolerance tests per ADA criteria.

## Evidence Sources

- **ADA Standards of Care 2024** — Primary guideline for screening criteria and risk stratification
- **USPSTF Recommendation 2021** — Grade B recommendation for screening adults 35-70 who are overweight/obese

## Evidence Grading

- Screening criteria: **High quality** (GRADE A — multiple RCTs)
- Risk factor thresholds: **Moderate quality** (GRADE B — observational cohorts)
